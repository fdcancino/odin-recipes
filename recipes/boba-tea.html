Boba Tea

Description

Enjoy this drink with a sweet surprise after every sip!

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Tapioca Balls
  • Ube flavour powder
  • Big Straw

Steps

  1. Add milk up to 3/4 of a glass
  2. Mix one scoope of Ube flavour into glass.
  3. Pour tapioca balls into glass until fluid is at the top.
  4. Enjoy!
